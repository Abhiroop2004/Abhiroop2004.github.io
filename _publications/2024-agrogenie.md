This study outlines the development of AgroGenie, an intelligent framework designed to modernize agricultural practices. Utilizing Random Forest and other machine learning techniques, AgroGenie analyzes environmental data—such as agricultural growth parameters—to offer actionable insights. This smart approach aids in crop recommendation and resource management, ultimately aiming to enhance crop yields and ecological sustainability.
